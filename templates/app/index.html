About ULK Polls

ULK Polls is an institutional voting platform designed specifically for Kigali Independent University. We provide a secure, transparent, and inclusive environment for the entire ULK community to participate in institutional decision-making processes.

Whether you're voting on student affairs, academic initiatives, or community matters, our platform ensures your voice is heard and counted. With real-time results and comprehensive analytics, we're committed to supporting a more engaged and informed university community.
